About Etaldegaule Geology Ltd

Your trusted partner in water & geology solutions.

Who We Are

Etaldegaule Geology Ltd is a leading water and geology company specializing in borehole drilling, geological surveys, hydrogeology, and high-quality water solutions. With over 1000 boreholes successfully drilled across multiple terrains, we stand as one of the most experienced and dependable drilling companies in the region.

Our Team

Our expert geologists, drill operators, and field engineers work with advanced modern tools to ensure accurate, durable, and high-yield water production. We combine science, experience, and field knowledge to deliver reliable water access for homes, farms, estates, and commercial projects.

Our Tools & Technology

Etaldegaule uses efficient geological equipment including rotary rigs, geophysical survey instruments, high-pressure compressors, and precision measurement tools. Our technology ensures fast drilling, accurate water detection, and long-lasting borehole output.

Our Impact

From rural communities to large urban projects, Etaldegaule has delivered consistent water solutions that improve quality of life, boost agriculture, and support sustainable development.

Our Mission

To provide safe, reliable, and sustainable water access through professional geological exploration and quality drilling.

Our Vision

To become Africa’s most trusted name in water and geology services, delivering innovative solutions powered by science and integrity.
